ACKNOWLEDGMENT OF REQUEST FOR RECONSIDERATION

We have received the request for reconsideration of our decision in connection with the procurement identified above.  Requests for reconsideration do not generally result in further development of the record, but the attorney assigned to the matter may request additional information if circumstances warrant.

Our Bid Protest Regulations provide that requests for reconsideration must contain a detailed statement of the factual and legal grounds upon which reversal or modification of the initial decision is deemed warranted, specifying any errors of fact or law, or information not previously considered by our Office.  4 C.F.R. § 21.14(a).  The repetition of arguments made during our consideration of the original protest and disagreement with our decision do not meet this standard.  Veda, Inc.--Recon., B-278516.3, B-278516.4, July 8, 1998, 98-2 CPD ¶ 12 at 4.  We have held that information not previously considered means information that was not available when the initial protest was filed.  Norfolk Dredging Co.--Recon., B-236259.2, Oct. 31, 1989, 89-2 CPD ¶ 405.

Requests for reconsideration are not subject to the 100-day statutory deadline for bid protests, but our Office endeavors to resolve them within that same timeframe.
